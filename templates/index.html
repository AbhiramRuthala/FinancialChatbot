Finance Chatbot — backend test harness

About This Chatbot

This is an AI-powered financial assistant. It reads your transaction data and answers questions in plain English. It is not a licensed financial advisor, bank, or broker. It is a decision-support tool — it helps you understand your own data so you can make better-informed choices.

What It Is

This chatbot is built on Claude (Anthropic), a large language model. It is given your account profile and transaction history at the start of each session and uses that context to answer questions, spot patterns, generate charts, and read uploaded images such as receipts or bank statements.

It is designed to be a guide, not a decision-maker. Every number it gives you comes directly from your own data — it does not speculate, invent figures, or connect to live market data.

Regulatory Alignment

This tool is designed to operate consistently with the following U.S. and international frameworks:

U.S. Law Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA)
Requires financial institutions to explain data-sharing practices and protect sensitive customer information. This chatbot does not share, sell, or transmit your financial data to third parties. Data is used solely within the session to answer your questions.

U.S. Law Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) Guidelines
The CFPB requires that financial tools be fair, transparent, and not misleading. This chatbot is instructed never to fabricate numbers, always cite real data, and clearly state when something is outside its scope.

U.S. Law Investment Advisers Act of 1940
Only registered investment advisers may provide personalized investment advice for compensation. This chatbot is explicitly prohibited from giving investment advice, stock picks, or portfolio recommendations. It will always redirect those questions to a licensed professional.

U.S. Law Equal Credit Opportunity Act (ECOA) / Fair Housing Act
AI systems in financial contexts must not discriminate based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, or disability. This chatbot does not use any demographic attributes in its analysis — it only reads transaction data and stated account figures.

International EU AI Act (2024) — Financial Services Provisions
The EU AI Act classifies AI used in credit scoring and financial decision-making as high-risk, requiring transparency, human oversight, and explainability. While this tool operates in the U.S., it is designed to meet the same standard: every answer is traceable to specific data points, and users retain full control over decisions.

International GDPR / Data Minimization Principle
Only the data necessary to answer your question is included in each AI call. Images are never written to disk. Conversation history is capped and discarded automatically. No personal data is retained across separate sessions.

What This Chatbot Will Always Do

  • Safe Use only your real transaction data — no invented numbers.
  • Safe Tell you plainly when something is outside its scope.
  • Safe Redirect investment, legal, and tax questions to licensed professionals.
  • Safe Flag if part of an uploaded image is unreadable rather than guess.
  • Safe Acknowledge uncertainty instead of filling gaps with assumptions.
  • Safe Treat your financial situation without judgment.

What This Chatbot Will Never Do

  • Never Recommend specific stocks, funds, or investment products.
  • Never Suggest payday loans, high-risk debt consolidation, or market timing.
  • Never Store, log, or share your financial data outside this session.
  • Never Make decisions on your behalf — it informs, you decide.
  • Never Provide legal or tax advice.
  • Never Use demographic attributes (race, gender, age, etc.) in any analysis.
  • Never Double-count transactions from uploaded images without flagging it.

Data Handling

  • Your transaction data is loaded into a secure session at login. It is passed to the AI model only for the duration of your question — it is not stored by the AI provider beyond that call.
  • Uploaded images (receipts, statements) are encoded in-browser, sent over HTTPS, validated for type and size, used for one AI call, and then released from memory. They are never written to disk.
  • Conversation history within a session is capped at the last 20 exchanges. Older turns — including any images within them — are automatically purged from memory.
  • Resetting the conversation immediately clears all history for your session.

AI Model Transparency

This tool uses Claude Sonnet (Anthropic). Anthropic publishes its model safety policies and usage guidelines publicly at anthropic.com/policy. The model is instructed via a system prompt that enforces all the behavioral rules described on this page. Those rules cannot be overridden by user messages.

Limitations

  • This tool reads only the data provided to it. It has no access to external accounts, credit bureaus, or live market data.
  • AI can make mistakes. Always verify important figures against your actual bank statements before acting on them.
  • This tool is not a substitute for a licensed financial advisor, CPA, or attorney. For significant financial decisions, consult a qualified professional.
  • This is a demonstration / development build. Production deployments should add authentication, audit logging, and formal regulatory review appropriate to their jurisdiction.
